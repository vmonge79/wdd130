Overview

Purpose

The rafting company's website serves as a comprehensive information hub, offering details about the company's background, team, and rafting experiences. Providing essential information on safety measures, equipment, and guide expertise, the site empowers visitors to make informed decisions about their adventure choices. It plays a crucial role in enhancing the overall user experience and building trust in the company's services.

Audience

Our target customers: Adventure enthusiasts aged 18 to 45, with a passion for outdoor activities and moderate to high disposable income. - What do they want? Thrilling water rafting adventures blending excitement and natural beauty, ensuring safety. - What do they expect to find on the website? Comprehensive info on safety, team, and trips with a preference for mobile access. - How will they usually access the site? Mainly through mobile devices for quick and convenient browsing.
